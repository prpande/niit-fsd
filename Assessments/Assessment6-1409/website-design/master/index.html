Dog Funny Event 2023

The 75th annual Dog Funny Event is being held in San Fransico City on September 1st. This is the funniest dog event of the year, starring over 300 different dogs. This event was made to show America how great dogs really are. There will be treats at the walk in, and for the dog owners there will be a buffet in the lounge. We thank you for coming to our event site, and hope to see you on September 1st. Thank you.
